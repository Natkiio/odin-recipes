Martian Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • 200g Dark Matter Chocolate
  • 100g Nebula Butter
  • 100g Stardust Crystals (sugar)
  • 4 Zargoth Eggs (green food coloring)
  • 50g Meteorite Flour

Instructions

  1. Melt Red Dwarf Chocolate and Cosmic Cream. Freeze in small molds.
  2. Melt Dark Matter Chocolate and Nebula Butter.
  3. Whisk Stardust Crystals and Zargoth Eggs.
  4. Combine with melted chocolate. Fold in Meteorite Flour.
  5. Grease 4 Hyperion Pods. Fill with batter, add frozen center, cover with batter.
  6. Bake at 200°C (390°F) for 12-15 minutes. Cool 1 minute.
  7. Serve anf enjoy!
