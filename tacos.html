Cosmic Galactic Tacos

Ingredients

Taco Shells

  • 8 Quasar Corn Discs
  • 1 teaspoon Nebula Dust (edible glitter)

Filling

  • 500g Ground Starbeast (ground beef)
  • 1 cup diced Meteorite Orbs (heirloom tomatoes)
  • 1/2 cup sliced Xylanthian Avocados (avocado with blue food coloring)
  • 1/2 cup chopped Zogwomp Fronds (red leaf lettuce)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped Zircon Onions (purple onions)
  • 1/4 cup shredded Plasma Cheese (cheddar cheese with edible glitter)

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup Milky Way Crema (sour cream with edible silver glitter)
  • 1 tablespoon Nebula Spice Blend (cosmic chili powder)
  • 1 teaspoon Quasar Garlic Essence (galactic garlic powder)
  • 1 teaspoon Stardust Salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Taco Shells:

    2. - Lightly toast Quasar Corn Discs in a pan until crisp.
    - Sprinkle Nebula Dust on the discs for a shimmering effect.

  2. Cook the Filling:

    3. - In a large skillet, cook Ground Starbeast over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
    - Add diced Meteorite Orbs and chopped Zircon Onions. Cook for another 5 minutes.

  3. Assemble the Tacos:

    4. - Fill each Quasar Corn Disc with the Starbeast mixture.
    - Top with Xylanthian Avocados, Zogwomp Fronds, and shredded Plasma Cheese.

  4. Make the Sauce:

    5. - In a small bowl, mix Milky Way Crema with Nebula Spice Blend, Quasar Garlic Essence, and Stardust Salt.

  5. Serve:

    6. - Drizzle the sauce over the tacos and enjoy your cosmic creation!
